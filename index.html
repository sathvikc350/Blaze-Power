About The Blooman

About

Blooman has proprietary nano-coating LFP & modified LFP (~10-15% Higher Density) low-cost Cathode materials architecture formulation, one-step process, and cells technology for electrification and renewable-based power grid requires stabilization of power flows.

Vision

High quality, dependable, Affordable, innovative battery components for future electrification and stationary storage.

Product

The first product is customized LFP and modified LFP cathode materials for EV, consumer, and stationary storage applications. In-house cathode materials production makes the Lithium cells build smoother, faster, and better.

R&D

Blooman Technology

Urgent climate-change threat requires global ramp-up of electrification across carbon- based sectors - Demand for energy storage has been rapidly increasing and various outfits have entered this nascent market, yet big gaps exist in terms of product line, quality, and reliability. At this juncture, Blooman looks to foray with Phosphate Cathode materials that are superior in performance, design, and other parameters. Leveraging its proprietary (License & Patent) Direct Amorphous Nano-Coating (DANC) particle technology perfected over years with focused R&D efforts, Blooman is confident of garnering significant market share.

About The Blooman

About

We are a lithium ion Advanced Cathode Materials & Cells technology company run by industry and academic R&D leaders.
Our strong understanding, in-depth, of sciences at the atomic level (Architecture, Crystallinity, single-phase) gives us spirit of grasping the essence of problem. While focusing on research and development , we capitalize on current Mechanisms. We constantly aim to improve core performance and achieve profitable production applying the cutting-edge-technology.

MISSION

TO CREATE THE SAFE / ENERGY-DENSE / FAST CHARGEING/COST EFFECTIVE CATHODE MATERIALS & CELLS THAT POWER A CLEAN AND SUSTAINABLE PLANET

VISION

To Build World Class Customized Batteries which Generates Energy and Power to Light the Planet Green

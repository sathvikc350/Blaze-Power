Blooman Believe that battery design is a key element to the overall performance of the device and therefore can influence the end user’s perception of the product as well as the manufacturer/brand. In an age of ever increasing computing-power driving more sophisticated technology, battery technology can be used to innovate and differentiate OEM devices under development and selecting the right rechargeable battery has become one of the most important factors for product developers in designing a successful product.
After a very long research BLOOMAN solves:-

  • NMP ratio :
    1. Determines the doping and thus the core formula for the Anode and Cathode.
    2. It is an intuitive process , depends on the cell composer, as he has to take into account multiple variables.
    3. We have had hardly any cell failures and have it on good account that some of the best companies too still struggle with coming up at the right proportions.
  • Electrolyte Selection:
    1. Organic Base electrolytes which are the industry standard lead to increased flammability. We have better alternatives that avoid this issue. Increases Safety.
  • Architecture Design :
    1. With 10 years of experience and patents under the belt, we win here hands down.
    2. Cell architecture is again an art , a delicate balance of NMP , Porosity, cell balancing, electrolyte solution, and stacking.
